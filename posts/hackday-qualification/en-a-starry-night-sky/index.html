[Hackday qualif] Osint - A starry night sky

March 13, 2023 · 1 min ·  
The large library within the Cancer sector contains many documents of all types and from all periods.
Among these documents, some have deteriorated over time to the point of finding only snippets of information.
This is for example the case of this image where we guess the words 9:00 p.m. and 2022.

Try to find the place and the month of shooting.

**Flag format: `HACKDAY{COUNTRY_MONTH}`** In English and in capitals !

Reversing the image we found a first localisation, it seems to be Elqui Valley in Chile. On the pictures we can identify an milky way wich is a Magellanic Cloud. On Wikipedia we can find https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_Magellanic_Cloud this pictures that help us to locate it with other constellations. Using the site https://www.timeanddate.com/astronomy/night/ and placing ourselves in chile at 9:00 pm we can try different month of the year and see which one correspond the better to let us see the milky way. Flag : HACKDAY{CHILE_MAY}
