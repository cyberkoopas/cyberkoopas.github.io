[Hackday final] Osint - My runaway daughter

May 13, 2023 · 2 min · 

This SOCMINT Osint challenge start with this post that gave us the Facebook profile of the missing daughter. On her profile nothing except her name and a picture. By googling her name we can find her LinkedIn. One of her LinkedIn post we can find her resume link on a Google Drive. Inspecting the element of the web page of the drive, we can find a crucial information to continue our investigation her email : julie.rufuberos07@gmail.com Using Epios.com on it, we can find all the public activity on this google account : We can find one of her review maps : We get our first flag by decoding the Base64 _HACKDAY{Clermont-Ferrand} Link to the maps review we have 2 pictures : In the text we learn that the postal card with the glass was taken in the first city she runaway by. So let’s reverse it : We get our second flag _HACKDAY{Vichy} Let’s continue our investigation we know for sure that she is a gamer and a developer, so she must have a Github profile. On it we can find a diary, where she wrote : That she visit her “favorite video game publisher”. On her LinkedIn she liked a lot of Ubisoft post.
